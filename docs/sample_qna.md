1. **Q:** What is the exit load for HDFC Small Cap Fund?
   **A:** The exit load for HDFC Small Cap Fund Direct Growth is 1% if redeemed within 1 year. *(Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/hdfc-small-cap-fund-direct-growth, Last updated from sources: 2025-11-16)*

2. **Q:** Expense ratio for HDFC Flexi Cap Fund Direct Plan?

3. **Q:** Lock-in period for HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund?
   **A:** HDFC ELSS Tax Saver Fund Direct Plan Growth carries a statutory 3-year lock-in on each investment. *(Source: https://groww.in/mutual-funds/hdfc-elss-tax-saver-fund-direct-plan-growth, Last updated from sources: 2025-11-16)*

4. **Q:** Who manages HDFC Flexi Cap Fund Direct Plan Growth?

5. **Q:** How do I download my capital-gains statement on Groww?
   **A:** Visit Groww → Investments → Statements → Capital Gains and export the financial year you need. *(Source: https://groww.in/blog/how-to-get-capital-gains-statement-for-mutual-fund-investments, Last updated from sources: 2025-11-16)*
